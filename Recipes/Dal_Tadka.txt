Dal Tadka

1 c toor dal
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 1/2 c water
4 cloves garlic
1 inch ginger
1 thing green chili
1 1/2 Tbsp ghee
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 Tbsp coriander seeds
2 cloves 
1 onion
2 tomatos
1/2 tsp coriander powder
1/4 tsp garam masala
1/4 tsp kashmiri red chili powder
1/8 tsp red chili powder
2 Tbsp chopped cilantro
1 tsp kasuri methi
2 tsp ghee
2 cloves garlic
1/4 tsp hing
2 things dried red chiles
1/4 tsp kashmiri red chili powder
--------
Cook dal with turmeric and 3 cups of water, either in pressure cooker or on stove.

Crush together 4 cloves garlic, 1 inch ginger, and green chili. Heat ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds, coriander seeds, and cloves. Saute for a few seconds, until fragrant.

Add onions, and cook for 3-4 minutes until soft and starting to change color. Add garlic-ginger-green chili, and cook for 1-2 more minutes until the raw smell goes away.

Add chopped tomatoes, along with 1/2 tsp salt. Cover and cook for 7-8 minutes until tomatoes are very soft and cooked. Add in coriander, garam masala, kashmiri chili powder, chili powder, cilantro, and kasuri methi. Stir in spices for 30 seconds.

Add the boiled dal to the pan, and mix. Add water as necessary if the dal looks too thick. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.

Make tadka: heat 2tsp ghee in a small pan. once hot, add 2 chopped garlic cloves, along with hing and dried red chiels. Cook for one minute until the garlic starts to change color. Add in kashmiri red chili powder and remove from heat.

Pour tadka over dal, and mix to combine. Garnish with cilantro and serve!
