Overnight Chocolate Chia Seed Pudding

1/4 c cocoa powder
4 Tbsp maple syrup
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 pinch sea salt
1 1/2 c almondmilk
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 c chia seeds
--------
Whisk the dry ingredients, maple syrup, and vanilla extract together. Add the almond milk, a little at a time, until a paste forms. Then add the remaining milk, and whisk until smooth. Add chia seeds, and whisk once more to combine.

Cover and refrigerate overnight, or at least 3-5 hours. It may be helpful to give the mixture an extra whisk once it has been chilling for 30-45 minutes.
