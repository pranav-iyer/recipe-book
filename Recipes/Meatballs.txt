Meatballs

1.0 lb meat
4.0 oz frozen spinach
1/2 c parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1.0 c breadcrumbs
2.0 eggs 
--------
Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Shape into small balls, and bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes. Check interior temperature and take out. Serve immediately.
